Scarlet Classic V

Scarlet Classic V was one of the largest competitive intercollegiate LANs on the east coast, hosted by Rutgers Esports. Entrant count racked up to over 950 total attendees. I designed the flyer and the on-site banner which included all of our partners for the event.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop CC

It's Just a Game

It's Just a Game was a charity and casual BYOC LAN event. Rutgers Esports teamed up with Extra-Life to help raise money for sick and injured children being treated in the Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. I designed the flyer for the event.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop CC

Fireside Open

Fireside Open was a competitive intercollegiate BYOC LAN event hosted by Rutgers Esports. A total of 700 attended the event. This was my first major event that I've ever been a part of and I was in charge of designing the flyer.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop CC

Rhythm Games Club

I created promotional materials such as the logo, flyer, and banner for the Rhythm Games Club. These images include the final product as well as some various draft ideas for the logo.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop CC, Adobe Illustrator CC
